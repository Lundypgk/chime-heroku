Frequently Asked Questions:



  1. 1. Who are Chimers exactly?

    Chimers are everyday people who share about things they’re passionate about including food, fashion, sports, arts, and anything else under the sun.

    Chimers have the freedom to be themselves and post content they genuinely care about. Therefore, their opinions are highly trusted by their followers, who generally consist of trusted friends and family.

    Create an account here to be our valued Chimer!

  2. 2. . Is it okay if I sign up without meeting the stated minimum requirements?

    We believe everyone has a voice and story to tell. We welcome anyone who is a passionate chimer so you can treat the requirements as a guideline.

  3. 3. Do Chimers have to pay to use Chime?

    There are no upfront or hidden fees when using Chime. It is 100% free. In fact, you will be rewarded with some cash for your effort in sharing about the things you love with your friend.

  4. 4. What benefits do I get as a Chimer?

    There are plenty of benefits that you can receive as a Chimer, like discounts on your food, free products, free services and many more. On top of that, you’ll receive a cash reward too.

  5. 5. How much cash reward will I get as a Chimer?

    Chime’s algorithm will calculate your cash reward according to the number of impressions that your Instagram account garner per post. You will be able to see your cash reward beside every brand listing.

  6. 6. When will I receive my cash reward?

    The cash reward will be deposited directly into your bank account approximately 5 days after you have successfully Chimed about the brand you love. Hassle free.

  7. 7. What kind of products and brands will I be able to Chime about?

    With the large variety of unique brands we work with, you can chime for just about any product or brand present in your city. The sky is the limit! We will do our best to suggest products and brands that we think suits your interests and personality

  8. 8. Can I choose which brand or product to Chime for?

    The cash reward will be deposited directly into your bank account approximately 5 days after you have successfully Chimed about the brand you love. Hassle free.

  9. 9. Am I allowed to delete my posts after Chiming for the brand?

    Being a Chimer is about sharing with others about things we like. Therefore, we highly encourage you to keep these Instagram pictures as they contain things you love or are truly passionate about – because that’s what makes you, you!

    However, we respect that you have autonomy over your own Instagram feed and thus, we strongly suggest that you keep these photos that you have Chimed for a reasonable period of time before deleting them

  10. 10. How does Chime handle the movement of cash rewards?

    The brands will firstly set a budget they wish to invest in advertising through Chime. This budget will be held by Chime in escrow. Chimers who successfully Chime for the brands they love will receive the budget in the form of cash rewards. Whereas Chime would take 15% of it as commission.
